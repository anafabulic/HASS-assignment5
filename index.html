How can we improve this?

Radical suggestion:

Bars convey magnitude better.

Interactivity reveals overlooked categories.

Simple channels put things into perspective.

Other fixes:

Make big numbers human-readable.


							d3.select(".tooltip-area")

									Area: ${ // convert sq m to sq km and add commas (00,000,000)

What have we learned?

↓

tl;dr:

  1. Some visualisation idioms may look pretty, but conceal important information.
  2. A simpler idiom with more interaction might be clearer than a dense one with lesser interaction.
  3. Don't blindly pull from backend—format the numbers first.
